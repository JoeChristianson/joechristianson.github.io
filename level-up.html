Vibe Energy Café is proud to announce their on-going fundraising opportunity, Level UP, to support local charitable organizations in the community.

Guests at Vibe Energy Café are invited to round up their purchase to the nearest dollar, with 100% of the proceeds being donated to a local charitable organization. In addition, on Fridays, Vibe Energy Café will donate 15% of all in-store proceeds to the chosen organization.

Vibe Energy Café is committed to giving back to the community and is excited to partner with local charitable organizations that make a positive impact. The Level UP campaign is an easy way for guests to learn about local organizations that positively impact their community and allow them to donate to a great cause, simply by rounding up their purchase to the nearest dollar. The in-store donation day will allow Vibe Energy Café to donate even more to the chosen organization and create a greater impact in the community.

Vibe Energy Café encourages everyone to visit the café to help make a difference in the community. Guests can also follow Vibe Energy Café on social media for updates on the chosen charitable organization and the impact of the fundraiser.

For more information, or if your charitable organization would like to participate as a chosen organization, please contact Vibe at erico@vibeenergycafe.com
